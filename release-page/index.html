Spendit

Overview

Spendit is a utility app to record everything you spend.

Notice

Since 0.2.x, Spendit supports registration with cloud syncing. However, due to limited server resources, you may register an account with invitation code only. Contact me to acquire one. Apparently, you have to find me first. :)

Features

  • Record your expenses everyday
  • View your expenses and summary
  • Organize with category, people and tags
